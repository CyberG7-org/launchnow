What Is LaunchNow?

A 7-Day Website Launch Service for Singapore SMEs

LaunchNow is a website design and launch service by CyberG7 Technologies. A LaunchNow Phase 1 website is a professional, mobile-responsive site of up to 5 pages with basic SEO and the first year of hosting, built and launched in 7 days for S$288. CyberG7 Technologies is a Singapore-based digital studio that designs, builds, and launches websites for startups and small businesses.
