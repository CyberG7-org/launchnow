Our Story

Built to Solve a Real Problem

CyberG7 Technologies was founded with a singular purpose: to solve the problem of overpriced web development for Singapore startups. We watched too many newly ACRA-registered businesses burn through their startup capital on websites that cost thousands — before they had even earned their first dollar.

We knew there had to be a better way. A way for every hawker stall, every consultancy, every first-time entrepreneur to get online without financial strain. So we built it.

As an ACRA-registered company ourselves, we understand the challenges Singapore SMEs face. Our mission from day one has been to make a professional web presence accessible to every business — regardless of budget.
